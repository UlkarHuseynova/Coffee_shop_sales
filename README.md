  While sales are relatively well-distributed throughout the week, there are some notable patterns:<br>
  Day of the Week: <br>
  The highest sales occur on Monday ($101,677) and Friday ($101,373).<br>
  The lowest sales volume is recorded on Saturday ($96,894).<br>
  Hour of the Day: Sales begin to rise sharply starting at 07:00 and reach their peak at 10:00 AM ($88,673). <br>
  After 11:00 AM, sales dip and remain steady ($40k–$41k) until the evening.<br>
