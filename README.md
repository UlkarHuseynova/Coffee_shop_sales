# Sales Analysis Report

## 1. Sales Variation by Day of the Week and Hour of Day
**Day of the Week:**
- **Highest sales:** Monday ($101,677) and Friday ($101,373)  
- **Lowest sales:** Saturday ($96,894)

**Hour of the Day:**
- Sales rise sharply from **07:00 AM**, peaking at **10:00 AM ($88,673)**  
- After **11:00 AM**, sales stabilize around **$40k–$41k**  
- **Peak sales window:** 08:00 AM – 10:00 AM  
- Specific high-revenue slots:  
  - Tuesday at 09:00 AM & 10:00 AM  
  - Friday at 10:00 AM
